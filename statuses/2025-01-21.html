--- title: 版本号数值膨胀一例（乐） ---

match-sorter是一个模糊搜索的JS库，在短短不到一个月的时间里主版本号增长了⅓。

6.4.0 (2024-10-16)

  • support finding data with typos (#153)

7.0.0 (2024-10-17)

  • release: The last release was arguably a breaking change so we're going to trigger this major version and deprecate the 6.4.0.

8.0.0 (2024-11-04)

  • revert: We've reverted the last release which added support for typos because it was too generous (#155)
